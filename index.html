About NeuroEnhance Solutions

NeuroEnhance Solutions (NES) is a pioneering enterprise dedicated to optimizing human cognitive function through the sustainable production of advanced omega-3 fatty acids. We cultivate alpha-linolenic acid (ALA) from cannabis and eicosapentaenoic acid (EPA) and docosahexaenoic acid (DHA) from seaweed to create a hybrid omega-3 product with unparalleled health benefits.

Our Products

NES offers a range of hybrid omega-3 products, combining ALA, EPA, and DHA from sustainable sources. These products are available in multiple forms, including:

  • Powder Form: Easy to mix with food or beverages
  • Liquid Form: Convenient for direct consumption
  • Capsule Form: Precise dosing and easy consumption

Market Overview

The global omega-3 market is experiencing significant growth driven by increasing consumer awareness of the health benefits of omega-3 fatty acids. NES targets key sectors like food and beverage manufacturers, nutritional supplements, and infant formula producers.

Key Market Trends:

  • Increased awareness of omega-3's role in preventing chronic diseases
